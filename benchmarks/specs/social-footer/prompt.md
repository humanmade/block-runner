# Social footer bar

A footer social bar: a short tagline paragraph "Built in the open.", then a row of three
social icon links — GitHub, X (Twitter), and LinkedIn — each linking out to that network.

Use real anchor links with recognisable social icons (the network is identifiable from the
href and/or icon). Output one self-contained HTML `<section>` or `<footer>`; use your own
CSS/classes.
